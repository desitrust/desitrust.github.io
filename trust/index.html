Trustees

PRASANNA

Founder Trustee

Prasanna is a distinguished theatre director and a social activist. He is a guest faculty at National School of Drama (NSD) and has served as director of Rangayana, Mysuru. He is the founder of Samudaya, Kavi-Kavya Trust, Charaka Women's Multipurpose Industrial Co-operative Society, Ontidani Prakashana and Desi Trust. He served as Managing Trustee till March 2013. He advises the Trust in almost all matters of decision making.

M S SATHYU

Trustee

M S Sathyu is a Film Director, Stage Designer, Dramatist and Art Director of national repute. He is a recipient of Padmashree in 1975. He has been part of Desi Trust since its inception.

JAYANTHI MARULASIDDAPPA

Trustee

Jayanthi Marulasiddppa, daughter of the well-known writer Shri. G S Shivarudrappa and wife of famous literary critic Shri. Marulasiddppa is a well-known costume designer for theatre and films. She is providing design service to Desi Trust as Trustee since inception.

J KRISHNA

Managing Trustee

J Krishna has worked at Charaka Womens' Co-operative and Desi Trust in various capacities. He is serving as Managing Trustee of Desi Trust since 2013 and currently also is the Head of Finance at Desi Trust.

PADMASHRI

Trustee

Padmashri is a member of the Charaka Womens' Co-operative where she oversees the Product Design and Textile development. She handles customer engagements for Charaka and also represents them at various exhibitions. She is a passionate theatre artist and pursues her talent in her free time.

AJAY KUMAR SINGH

Trustee

Ajay Kumar Singh served the Karnataka State Police Department as Director General and Inspector General of Police (DG&IGP). He is also a distinguished writer. After his retirement he is actively involved in social activities and advises the Trust.

M S KESHAVA

Trustee

M S Keshava is the Director of Chayagraphics India Pvt. Ltd. for the last 25 years and also Director of Prognosys Medical Systems Pvt. Ltd. for the last 10 years. He is senior partner of VK Commerce for the last two decades. He advises the Trust on financial matters.

N RAMESH

Trustee

N Ramesh is working in Charaka Womens' Co-operative since last 17 years in various capacities, including serving as Secretary of the Co-operative. He acts as a liaison between Charaka Society and Desi Trust.

MAHALAKSHMI

Trustee

Mahalakshmi has been a member of Charaka Womens' Co-operative since its inception. She has served as the Secretary of the co-operative and also as one of the board of Directors in the past. Currently she is in charge of the Pre-loom and Loom activities.

MADHURA

Trustee

Madhura has been a member of Charaka Womens' Co-operative for over two decades. She has served as a Director in the co-operative and currently in charge of the Post-loom production activities.

M V CHANDRASHEKAR

Trustee

M V Chandrashekar is Fashion Designer who runs his own studio named Metaphor at Bengaluru. He is involved in costume designing for Films and Television. He has been Trustee of Desi since its inception and is also the Chief Designer at Charaka Womens' Co-operative.

K T CHIKKANNA

Trustee

K T Chikkanna has served in Kannada and Culture Dept. of Karnataka Govt. at various capacities and retired as Joint Director. He is a famous writer in Kannada and presently works as Co-ordianator of the Kanakadasa Peeta of State Govt. He advises the Trust in various policy matters.

SHASHIDHAR ADAPA

Trustee

Shashidhar Adapa is a Stage designer and an Art Director for Indian films. He contributes his skills towards social causes and helps design the venue for various movements. He has been a Trustee since 2018.

SHARADA GANESH

Trustee

Sharada heads the Planning and Sustainability Department at Desi Trust. She advises the Trust on various initiatives including production planning, development projects, content, digital design and systems. She volunteers with various environmental and wildlife causes and organizations working in the field of ecological sustainability.
