Products

At Desi we have over 100 categories of products hand woven and hand crafted by artisans in our villages.

By choosing our product you will join the ever-growing family of responsible consumers who believe in rural empowerment, sustainability and traditional art. Visit one of our stores to get the Desi experience and choose from a range of products - traditional sarees from Ilkal to handmade toys from Channapatna.
