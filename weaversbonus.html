Beyond the renumeration.

DESI ensures that the artisan's hard work is rewarded appropriately

Bonus for weavers

Every year, based on the amount of production achieved, each artisan (weaver, pre-loom, post-loom, admin staff, helpers) are provided bonus during the Deepavali festival. DESI staff travel to the remote villages to personally thank each artisan and hand over the bonus to them. This is a way to acknowledge their performance and also provide encouragement for them to produce better quantity and quality in the coming months.

DESI also provides development funds to Charaka for its various projects and helps Charaka implement the projects.
